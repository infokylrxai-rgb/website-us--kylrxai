Why Choose Kylrx AI?

Enterprise-grade HR automation built for the modern workforce

📊

Real-Time Employee Monitoring

Gain complete and immediate visibility into daily attendance logs, active hours, and departmental productivity metrics. Our real-time monitoring system features instant anomaly alerts, mismatch notifications, and continuous operational health checks to keep managers perfectly informed.

🔒

Enterprise Security & Scalability

Protect sensitive employee records and payroll ledgers with bank-grade AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest. Backed by Single Sign-On (SSO) integration, secure custom role-based claims, and high-fidelity database synchronization, your enterprise data is always secure, audit-ready, and highly available.

💸

Automated Payroll & Taxes

Compute complex payroll, custom tax brackets, and mandatory statutory benefits with absolute precision. Our unified engine automatically calculates tax deductions, Provident Fund contributions, professional taxes, and initiates secure, single-click direct bank transfers in seconds.

📅

Custom Rostering & Scheduling

Design and publish complex, multi-shift rosters and department calendars in seconds. Our smart rostering system automatically factors in weekly rests, custom shifts, and local holidays while instantly alerting supervisors about roster overlaps and resource shortages.

💬

Employee Portal Messenger

Accelerate employee response times with an integrated, secure messaging system. Employees can directly submit leave requests, raise payroll queries, and receive instant automated updates on shifts and company policies directly inside their unified portal.

🛡️

Trust & Compliance Audit

Maintain continuous, compliance-level oversight across your entire workforce. Track employee background screening statuses, verify legal documents, and access instant, audit-ready compliance reports to completely eliminate regulatory risks, labor violations, and auditing overhead.
